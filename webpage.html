Business

  • Oil Prices on the Rise: Libya's oil exports are at a near standstill due to political infighting over control of the nation’s central bank. This is causing market jitters and pushing global oil prices up, adding pressure to an already tight energy market as the world anticipates continued disruptions​( The Cipher Brief).
  • Tech Layoffs Continue: Major tech firms, including some Silicon Valley giants, are continuing rounds of layoffs as they adjust to post-pandemic demand levels. The sector is also seeing a cooling off in venture capital funding as investors become more cautious amid interest rate concerns​( The Cipher Brief).
